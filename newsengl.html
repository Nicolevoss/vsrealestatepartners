A TREASURE IN THE MEDITERRANEAN

Experience the beautiful nature of Northern Cyprus

A paradise of untouched beauty

Welcome to Northern Cyprus—considered one of the last unspoiled paradises in the Mediterranean. Far from mass tourism, you will find breathtaking natural landscapes, picturesque bays, and an incredible variety of plant and animal species. Northern Cyprus is a place where nature can still be experienced in its purest form. Clean air, crystal-clear waters, and expansive beaches invite you to unwind.

A safe haven for endangered sea turtles

Particularly impressive is Northern Cyprus’s role as one of the most important nesting sites for endangered sea turtles. The region’s beaches—including Alagadi Beach and Karpaz Golden Beach—are so clean and protected that they provide ideal conditions for the loggerhead turtle (Caretta caretta) and the green sea turtle (Chelonia mydas) to lay their eggs. Each year these majestic animals return to the shores to nest—a remarkable natural spectacle that you can even observe at night with expert guidance.

A fascinating flora and fauna

Beyond sea turtles, Northern Cyprus is home to exceptional wildlife. You can observe rare bird species, including flamingos that rest in the region’s salt lakes during the winter months. Wild donkeys roaming freely on the Karpaz Peninsula are a symbol of the island’s unspoiled nature. Dense forests and meadows provide a habitat for countless butterflies and other insects that bring the landscape to life.

Blooming landscapes and unique scents

Northern Cyprus is also a paradise for plant lovers. The region is known for its wildflowers, which are in full bloom in spring. In particular, the wild orchids that grow here attract nature enthusiasts from all over the world. The air is filled with the scent of pine forests and citrus trees, making your visit unforgettable.

A place of peace and relaxation

The nature of Northern Cyprus not only invites exploration but also offers ideal conditions for rest and relaxation. Far from hustle and noise, you can relax on secluded beaches, hike through picturesque landscapes, or cycle along the coastal roads. Northern Cyprus is a place to recharge and enjoy nature’s beauty in its purest form.
