Experience the Beautiful Nature of Northern Cyprus

A Paradise of Untouched Beauty

Welcome to Northern Cyprus – a region considered one of the last untouched paradises in the Mediterranean. Away from mass tourism, you will find breathtaking natural landscapes, picturesque bays, and an incredible variety of plant and animal species. Northern Cyprus is a place where nature can still be experienced in its purest form. The clean air, crystal-clear sea, and expansive beaches invite you to unwind and relax.

A Safe Haven for Endangered Sea Turtles

One of the most impressive aspects of Northern Cyprus is its role as one of the most important nesting sites for endangered sea turtles. The beaches of the region, including Alagadi Beach and Karpaz Golden Beach, are so clean and protected that they provide ideal conditions for the nesting of Loggerhead turtles (Caretta caretta) and Green sea turtles (Chelonia mydas). Every year, these majestic creatures return to the beaches to lay their eggs – an impressive natural spectacle that you can even observe at night with expert guidance.

A Fascinating Flora and Fauna

In addition to the turtles, Northern Cyprus is home to an extraordinary array of wildlife. You can observe rare bird species here, including flamingos, which stop in the region's salt lakes during the winter months. Wild donkeys, living freely on the Karpaz Peninsula, are a symbol of the untouched nature of Northern Cyprus. The dense forests and meadows provide habitat for a variety of butterflies and other insects that bring the landscape to life.

Blooming Landscapes and Unique Scents

Northern Cyprus is also a paradise for plant lovers. The region is known for its wildflowers, which bloom in full in spring. Especially the wild orchids that grow in this area attract nature lovers from all over the world. In addition, the air is filled with the scent of pine forests and citrus trees, making your visit an unforgettable experience.

A Place for Peace and Relaxation

The nature of Northern Cyprus not only invites exploration but also offers ideal conditions for peace and relaxation. Away from the hustle and bustle, you can unwind on deserted beaches, hike through picturesque landscapes, or explore the coastal roads by bike. Northern Cyprus is a place where you can recharge your energy and enjoy the beauty of nature in its purest form.
