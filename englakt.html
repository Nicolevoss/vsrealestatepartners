Experience the Activities of Northern Cyprus

Swimming and Relaxing on Stunning Beaches

Northern Cyprus is a paradise for beach lovers. The region offers miles of sandy beaches where you can soak up the sun or swim in the crystal-clear waters of the Mediterranean. The Karpaz Golden Beach, one of the longest and most beautiful beaches on the island, is especially popular for swimming and relaxing. Away from the main beaches, you can find hidden coves that guarantee peace and privacy. The warm waters also invite you to snorkeling adventures, where you can explore the fascinating underwater world.

Exploring Historic Cities

Northern Cyprus boasts a rich history that you can experience firsthand on tours of its charming cities and villages. Visit Girne (Kyrenia), known for its picturesque harbor and impressive fortress, or Famagusta, a city full of historical treasures like the Lala Mustafa Pasha Mosque. The ancient city of Salamis, with its well-preserved Roman ruins, is also a must-see for history enthusiasts.

Cycling Along the Coast

For cycling enthusiasts, Northern Cyprus offers numerous routes along the coast or through the mountainous hinterland. The well-maintained paths allow you to explore the island's beauty on two wheels. Along the way, you can stop at small caf√©s or viewpoints to enjoy breathtaking panoramas.

Boat Tours and Water Sports

Discover Northern Cyprus from the water! Boat tours along the coast let you explore hidden coves, sea caves, and remote beaches. Additionally, you can try various water sports, including kayaking, windsurfing, or diving. Diving excursions give you the chance to explore shipwrecks and the fascinating underwater world of the region.

A Destination for Everyone

Whether you're an adventurer, a history buff, or just looking for relaxation, Northern Cyprus offers a wide range of activities to make your stay unforgettable. Combine exploration tours with relaxing days on the beach and experience the versatility of this unique region.
