WHY NORTHERN CYPRUS?

Discover these valuable facets of a unique island

  • High season lasts 9 months, with steadily growing tourist traffic
  • Lower prices compared with other Mediterranean real estate
  • Rental returns of 8–15% per year, alongside steady asset value growth
  • Flexible payment options in multiple currencies, including cryptocurrencies
  • Freehold land ownership when purchasing the property
  • English education and legal system
  • High standard of medical care
  • Regional food production with a ban on genetic modification
