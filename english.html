WHY NORTHERN CYPRUS?

Discover a unique island

Northern Cyprus is a true Mediterranean gem, attracting not only with its stunning nature but also its historical significance and many opportunities for leisure. Whether you want to enjoy the beauty of nature, immerse yourself in history, or just relax on the beach, Cyprus offers something for everyone. Especially Northern Cyprus has become an emerging region that offers you the perfect combination of tranquility, history, and modern amenities with 300 sunny days per year.

Here are some values that await you in Northern Cyprus:

  • High tourist flow, peak season lasts 9 months
  • Low prices compared to other Mediterranean real estate markets
  • Guaranteed rental income of 8-15% per year, along with steady property value growth
  • Flexible payment systems in different currencies, including cryptocurrency
  • Land ownership rights when purchasing property
  • English educational system and legal framework
  • High standard of healthcare services
  • Local food production with a ban on genetically modified organisms
